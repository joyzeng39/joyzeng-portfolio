Playing Card App


I created a set of fruit inspired playing cards as well an app prototype for the cards to be displayed in. The app allows the user to scroll through their cards and view each in full view. The user can also add and delete cards, but can never have more than six cards or less than one card. The user can also filter and sort their cards in the side menu.
