Design is Organic


Design is a different for everyone. The process of design, the final product, the initial vision all represent who we are as people and what we gravitate towards. To me - design is an organic process filled with overlapping and contradicting ideas. Design is a jumbled mess. Until it isn't. For this poster concept, I wanted to represent organized mess. Design is oraganic, but it is also refined and purposeful.
