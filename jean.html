More than Workwear


Jeans were invented with purpose of being tough and rugged workwear. In the 1950s, jeans started to become a staple fashion piece skyrocketed in popularity. This poster celebrates the invention, but also the development of jeans as fashion rather than just function. I created two iterations of this poster and played around with textures to create a patchwork feel while still maintaining integrity as a poster.
